Welcome to Black Jack. Here are the basic rules.
Your goal is to get as close to the number 21 as possible without going over 21. Jack, Queen, King are equal to 10.
Ace is equal to 11 unless you go over 21, in which case it becomes 1.
If you think you're too low, you can click 'Hit' to add a card to your side of the table and to your score.
Once you find a number you're happy with, hit 'Stand'. This means you don't want to draw anymore.
The Dealer cannot 'Hit', or Draw a card, if he is above 16.
Good luck.
